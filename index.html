The Impact of Coral Bleaching

Welcome to Coral Beat

Our focus is on Hawaii and her coral reefs. The value of these reefs to the people of Hawaii for physical sustenance and deep cultural connections as well as a draw for the tourism economy cannot be overestimated. We offer this resource for local residents, visitors, and the science community to monitor the short and long-term effects of bleaching.

2015 Sea Surface Temperature

2015 was hot! The average global temperature was the highest ever recorded. Cyclical climactic ocean events, such as El Nino, brought warm waters further north and stressed reefs worldwide. This interactive map of 2015 sea surface temperature shows the heart-beat of seasonal change.
